Letters

This page list my letters to magazine that have been printed.

Nintendo Power, volume 254, May 2010, Pulse, page 7.

Always in Style

Blaster Master was too hard for me when I was young. As a full-grown adult I thought I would use Blaster Master Overdrive as some sort of late-bloomer rite of passage, but the first boss is quite the challenge. I love it!
- FLUX RIDER

Answer from the editor:
I am loving the return of all these retro games and franchises on WiiWare. Blaster Master Overdrive, Megaman 9 and 10, Sonic 4, Contra, Gradius, and Castlevania ReBirth—what a great time for fans of the classics!

Nintendo Power, volume 247, November 2009, Pulse, page 8.

Q. What do you think of Wii Sports Resort and Wii MotionPlus?

A. The Wii MotionPlus accessory adds new possibilities, but does not resolve much of the imprecision that is common to all motion-controlled games. My favorite Wii titles are still the button-based ones such as Mega Man 9, Punch-Out!!, and Fire Emblem. Nothing beats the tried and true, 100-percent-precise buttons.
- FLUX RIDER
