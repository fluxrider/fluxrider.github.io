Magic Number

Game description

Magic Number is a one-of-a-kind addictive game that will keep you awake on those long sleepless nights. The game features the never-tired Random Joe, Binary Jack and Risky Bob. You must choose an avatar to battle these terrific foes in the endless battle of revealing the magic number. To prevail, you must understand the twisted minds of your crazy opponents and then thwart their next moves!

Versions

Magic Number was first created on August 16, 1999, and later remade in September 2002.
